Instructions - Capturing videos of objects with a cellphone
  1. Take an orange and place it on a flat surface (e.g. a table).
  2. Take your phone and record a video of the object going round it from all sides.
  3. Click on Choose file below to select the video from your phone.
  4. Click on the Submit button below to finish the HIT. Please wait for submitting to finish before leaving the page as uploading the video may take a long time.
Avoid capturing any people or objects with trademarks (e.g. a Coca-Cola can).
For bonus reward:
  • Print the following QR marks (on letter paper) and put them on the flat surface around the object.
    • mark1, mark2, mark3
  • Make sure that at least 2 marks are visible at any moment.
  • Do not put two marks of the same type into one video.
  • Please retain the three pieces of paper for possible future tasks.
